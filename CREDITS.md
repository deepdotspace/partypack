## Sound Effects

All sound effects (click, pop, whoosh, drumroll, ding, stamp, fanfare,
countTick, buzzer) are **synthesized at runtime** using the Web Audio API
(oscillators, gain envelopes, noise buffers). No third-party SFX assets are
included or required.
